The Lore

Cat Quest

The old masters, often regarded as a fairy tale, have vanished and left the land filled with ruins. They appear to have had advanced magical and/or technological capabilities; they could record videos that would only be played when stumbled upon by a dragonblood. What they normally did and what happened to them is unknown.
One day, dragons, driven away from their home by danger, made Felingard their new home. According to legend, they terrorized the place, making it hard to live there. According to one of the few remaining dragons, the opposite was true. They were kind to those they met, and even taught the old masters about magic! One old master in particular, Aelius, managed to infuse cats with dragon magic; he called these cats the Dragonblood. Aelius loved his Dragonbloods, and one in particular, Drakoth, loved him very much. When the old masters grew fearful of Aelius and sealed him away, Drakoth vowed to free him one day.

And so the game begins. You, the hero, are sailing on a boat with your sister when a forboding white cat appears before you, and with his magic powers, kidnaps your sister without approaching your boat. He then uses more magic to destroy your boat, leaving you lost at sea. Luckily, Spirry, a guardian spirit was watching and came to help you not just reach land, but also to rescue your sister from the evil paws of the white cat named Drakoth.

Cat Quest II

Once, there was a powerful blade which the kingdom of cats and the kingdom of dogs were fighting over like, well, cats and dogs. Then one day, the blade shattered and their world was never the same again. As a great evil reigned, the kings of the two kingdoms fought against it, but eventually fell victim to its corruption. A prophecy foretold that one day, the kings would return, and that is where this game begins. After being informed that their thrones have been taken by evil kings named Lioner the Purrsecutor and Wolfen the Labrathor, the freshly reincarnated kings vow to help the subjects they love and care for, and so their adventure begins.

Cat Quest: Pirates of the Purribean

As this game has not been released yet, not much is known about the lore surrounding it. According to the trailer, your character is a feline pirate trying to find an ancient relic before those searching for it to bring about the Apawcalypse.

The Developers

The Cat Quest franchise is made by a small group of developers called The Gentlebros. According to their website, Cat Quest was inspired by other video games. In fact, Cat Quest has "the overworld of Final Fantasy, the combat and exploration of Zelda, and the open world of Skyrim all in one package". The only game mentioned on their website that is not a member of the Cat Quest franchise is Slashy Hero. The specific devs who worked on the Cat Quest games are as follows:
