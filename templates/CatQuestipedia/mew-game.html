{{ game }}: Mew Game

Mew Game is a way to play the game that the player unlocks after completing the main game. In this feature, players can add rules or 'meowdifiers' to a new playthrough of the game. Mew Game is meant to be a challenge mode, so most meowdifiers make the game harder. This page tracks the various meowdifiers a player can impose on their new playthrough. The amount of meowdifiers you impose on your playthrough affect the rewards you get for completing the run.{% if game == 'Cat Quest' %} In Cat Quest, you will get a different equipment depending on whether you have 1, 2, or 3+ meowdifiers on your run. There is, however, a mode in Cat Quest that will make the game easier and negate any rewards you will get.{% elif game == 'Cat Quest II' %} In Cat Quest II, the number of meowdifiers you impose on yourself affect the level of the piece of equipment you get.{% endif %}
